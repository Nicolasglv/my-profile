✈My Travels I enjoy exploring other cultures, meeting new people, and so forth. Seeing the globe, in my opinion, is more educational than attending high school or college. When we travel, we have the opportunity to acquire a new language, a new cuisine, parts of a foreign culture, and perhaps a greater understanding of faith or spirituality.
Also, I believe that traveling opens your mind. You learn there is no one-size-fits-all approach to life. Meeting individuals from other regions, in particular, will teach you that your worldview is not the same as everyone else's.

DRAWING Drawing has helped me express my emotions and let out everything that has been running through my head. I find it really satisfying to simply pick up a brush or a pencil and begin experimenting with ideas that come to mind. In addition, I believe that drawing has helped me relax and focus about other things when I'm stressed.
I used to draw a lot when we had art classes in school, but once we stopped having them, I stopped doing it. However, I still use drawing as a kind of therapy, fulfillment, and self-expression from time to time. My zodiac sign is Aquarius, and my favorite movie is "The Matrix," so I prefer to draw technical and spacey stuff a lot.
